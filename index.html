Meeting format

Our usual meeting format is that two or three members give prepared speeches of their own choosing.

These commonly last 6 to 8 minutes each.

There is also a Topics Session during which members have the opportunity to make a short 2 or 3 minute impromptu speech on allocated, often topical, subjects.

All speakers are given friendly, helpful feedback, in a positive way, by experienced fellow members.
