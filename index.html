President's Address

To our valued members present and future,

Just thinking over the past year and the really difficult situation we found ourselves in.

On the plus side we were able to continue our speakers meetings by using zoom.

We will get together again in whatever format in October but in the meantime can I thank you all for your contributions over the season and wish you a good summer.

The great advantage of zoom is that we found we can readily have joint meetings with other clubs, which we did frequently. This brings forward the idea that our future club house meetings could also include zoom meetings. Your views on this idea are welcome.

We all regretted missing out on our 75th anniversary dinner, our Burns supper and the social aspects such the pub quizzes, all of which provide the camaraderie we have.

I am looking forward to when we can meet again and that we will all be able to commit to coming back to the club.

We will have a committee meeting soon to discuss the way forward.

Keep well and stay safe and let's look forward to seeing each other again soon. 

Yours in friendship

Chris Robertson
